Integra

A web-based management system designed to be used in health sector.

June, 2021

Integra offers a wider accessibility and digitization of medical information, with an improved user experience based on current needs.

Applied technologies: Javascript, React, Redux, Material UI, Firebase, Supabase.

Main features:
  • Different user roles: administrative, doctor and affiliate.
  • Different CRUDs (Create, Read, Update and Delete experience) based on user role.
  • Login authentication.
  • Chatbot service.
  • Automatic email sending.
  • Mercado Pago integration.
  • Zip code is automatically populated based on selected city and state.
  • Instead of orders, prescriptions and instructions handwriting, doctors are able to generate/print them, making them easier to be understood by pharmacists, non specialized personnel as secretaries and patients.
  • Required data fields, including patient information, prescriber registration number and date among others, are automatically populated by the system.
  • Once printed, the order/prescription is hand-signed by the doctor.
  • Notes with detailed instructions are sent by email to the patient.
  • Patients can have a detail of previous consultations, prescriptions and medical studies.
  • A doctor may access a patient medical record, including diagnosis, analysis and studies written by other colleagues when asked for consultation or second opinion on a particular symptom or illness.
Wishlist for future possible implementations:
  • Telehealth consultation by streaming
  • Token for signature authentication

NOTE: This was a group integrative project presented as part of the evaluative instance of Henry bootcamp.
